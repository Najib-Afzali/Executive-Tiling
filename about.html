Who we are

Precision is the whole job

Executive Tiling is a Christchurch-based tiling company working across homes, renovations, commercial fit-outs and townhouse developments throughout Canterbury. We built the business around a simple idea: a tiled room and a finished room are not the same thing.

The difference comes down to what happens before the first tile is set — substrate checked, set-out planned on paper, falls and movement joints worked out so the result reads clean from every angle. We carry waterproofing and tiling under one trade, so there's a single point of responsibility and no gaps between hands.

For builders and developers, that means consistency at volume and a programme we actually hold to. For homeowners, it means a premium finish and straight answers from start to handover.

How we work

Four stages, one standard

Step 01

Consult & set-out

We assess the space, confirm tile and layout, and plan set-out so cuts fall where they should and lines run true.

Step 02

Prepare & waterproof

Substrate is corrected and wet areas are waterproofed to code — documented and warrantable before any tile goes down.

Step 03

Tile & finish

Tiles are laid flat with controlled lippage, tight grout lines and clean silicone — the details that define the finish.

Step 04

Clean handover

The area is cleaned down and checked against the standard we set on day one, then handed back ready to use.
